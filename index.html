What to notice

The traffic light itself does not contain one universal action. The meaning comes from combining:

Signal + Role + Rules → Action

This app demonstrates how perspective and context can affect interpretation. It does not represent every real traffic law or situation. Real roads include additional signals, signs, and safety rules.
